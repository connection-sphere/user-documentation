## What's DFY vs. SaaS?  
- **DFY = "Done For You"** (You relax, we do the work.)  
- **SaaS = "Software-as-a-Service"** (You drive, we give you the car.)  

**TL;DR:**  
- **DFY** = Full-service (like a personal assistant).  
- **SaaS** = Self-service (like a DIY toolkit).
